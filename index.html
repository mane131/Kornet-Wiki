The Old Roblox Revival

Overview

Kornet is an old Roblox revival aiming to recreate the feel of classic Roblox gameplay and community interaction. It preserves legacy styles, mechanics, and aesthetics from earlier versions of Roblox. It was founded in Nov 7 2025 leading up to today when this wiki was created.

The main hub for the community is the Discord server, where members interact, get updates, and participate in revival-related events.

Official Discord Server: https://discord.gg/u5En32wvwM

What Is an Old Roblox Revival?

An old Roblox revival is a community project (like an offline client or website) that aims to bring back the nostalgic experience of playing Roblox from its earlier years (roughly 2006-2013). These revivals often feature the older blocky graphics, simple UIs, classic games.
